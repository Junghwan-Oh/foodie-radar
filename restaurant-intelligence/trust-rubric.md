# Trust Rubric

Last updated: 2026-05-18

## Scores

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| S | Multiple strong layers agree and recent feedback is positive. |
| A | Strong direct evidence with no major risk signals. |
| B | Promising but incomplete; recommend conditionally. |
| C | Discovery candidate only; stale or weak evidence. |
| D | Avoid for now; conflict, closure, ad opacity, or repeated misses. |

## Advertising Risk Signals

- High: explicit ad/sponsorship/gift/invite/campaign/affiliate or scripted praise.
- Medium: ambiguous invite language, frequent brand deals, or owner relationship.
- Low: self-paid/revisit/concrete criticism/price details/recent independent corroboration.

## High-Value Recommendation Signals

These signals increase restaurant priority only when the source is a trusted creator and the content includes concrete menu/order details.

| Signal | Score Effect | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| 해당 분야 최고 / 국내 1위 / 장르 1위 | +3 | Strong category-peak signal. Best when from comparison or ranking content. |
| 인생맛집 / 자주 가는 단골 / 반복 방문 | +3 | Stronger than a one-off visit, especially if self-paid. |
| 무조건 와봐야 할 경험 | +2 | Strong experiential signal; still requires menu-level evidence. |
| 극강의 가성비 | +2 | Only counts when quality is also high. Cheap-only is not enough. |
| 장인의 퀄리티 / 전문 장르 장인 | +2 | Craft, specialization, and consistency matter. |
| Concrete criticism and ordering advice | +2 | More useful than praise because it helps avoid wrong orders. |

## Creator Removal / Downgrade Signals

| Signal | Action |
|---|---|
| Generic promotion or marketing-heavy content | Downgrade or remove from main source pool. |
| Frequent sponsorship without concrete criticism | Move to advertising caution or discovery-only. |
| Viral/hot-place content without dish-level detail | Keep as weak discovery only. |
| Taste mismatch after repeated user visits | Downgrade taste-fit even if source is popular. |
