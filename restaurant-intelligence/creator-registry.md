## Lifecycle Rules

- Add newly hot creators to `creator-lifecycle.md` as `hot-candidate` before trusting them.
- Promote to `active` only after official-channel audit and sponsorship/disclosure review.
- Move to `probation` when user feedback, public feedback, or sponsorship patterns weaken trust.
- Move to `inactive` or `archived` when a creator stops producing useful current food content.
- Move to `excluded` when repeated evidence shows hidden ads, generic hype, or poor recommendation quality.
- Delete only duplicates, mistaken identities, spam, or clearly out-of-scope entries; otherwise archive.
